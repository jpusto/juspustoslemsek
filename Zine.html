Zine

In the Photography course, I participated in the project "Cosmic Anarchism," where students created their own unique contributions. My piece was a zine in which I used photomontages to interpret several poems by Anton Podbevšek from the collection "Človek z bombami." I focused on dynamism, creative composition, and linking visual elements with the context of the poems.
