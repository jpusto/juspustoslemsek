Izven okvirja

In the Illustration class, four of us students designed a book titled The Shadow Cabinet. The book's theme focuses on political control, which we metaphorically depicted through illustrations using the collage technique. The emphasis was on dynamism, playfulness, and irony, while striving to maintain the clarity and purpose of the message. The image shows one spread and the book's cover.
