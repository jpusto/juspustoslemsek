Misel

As part of my diploma thesis, I created an abstract animation designed as a visual interpretation of music. The three-minute animation is crafted to visually mimic and enhance musical elements. My project was also recognized at the international design competition Graphis, where it received a gold award. The full animation can be viewed at: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aeJvkrpcGqc.
