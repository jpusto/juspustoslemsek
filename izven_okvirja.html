Izven okvirja (Outside the Frame)

After winning an internal competition at the Academy of Fine Arts and Design, the three of us—students—designed the visual identity for the exhibition "Outside the Frame" at Cankarjev dom. The project was extensive and included designing the catalog, animations, posters, social media materials, and other promotional content. Our goal was to create a visually coherent and appealing identity that reflects the uniqueness of the exhibition
